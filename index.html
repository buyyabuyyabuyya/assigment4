Deep Space Exploration

Ever since I was a young child looking at the stars in the back yard through my old low quality telescope; I have had an absolute obsession for all things space. The James Webb Space Telescope has taken this passion to an entirely new level. This telescope is located in the extreme cold of outer space, and is able to "see" past thick clouds of cosmic dust to determine the exact process by which stars form.

For more information on the latest discoveries, you can visit the official NASA Webb Telescope page.
